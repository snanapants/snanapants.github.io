About Me

I am a dedicated intellectual property lawyer and researcher with extensive experience in copyright, trademark, and emerging technology law. Currently pursuing my LL.M. in Intellectual Property at the University of Turin in collaboration with WIPO, I specialize in the intersection of artificial intelligence and intellectual property rights.

My research focuses on adapting traditional IP frameworks to contemporary challenges, particularly in music licensing, AI-generated works, and gender disparities in creative sectors. With a strong background in both practical legal work and academic research, I bring a comprehensive understanding of intellectual property law to every project.

I am passionate about helping creators, innovators, and businesses navigate the complex landscape of intellectual property protection while staying at the forefront of legal developments in technology and creative industries.
